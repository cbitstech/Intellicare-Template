Why Be Grateful?

Gratitude is good for our bodies, hearts, and minds. Gratitude is associated with many social, physical, and psychological benefits. These benefits are available to anyone who practices gratitude, even in the middle of difficulties and struggles. Actually, gratitude is especially important when times get tough.

Throughout the week, you'll receive instructions, advice, and reminders to practice gratitude in new and different ways each day. If you go through the motions and practice, the feeling of gratitude will come. So try it out this week, even when you don't feel like it, and notice how it changes how you feel and what you do!
