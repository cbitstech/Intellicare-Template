An Attitude of Gratitude

"Be thankful for what you have; you'll end up having more. If you concentrate on what you don't have, you will never, ever have enough."

Gratitude has two parts. First, through gratitude you affirm that there are good things in the world, as well as gifts and benefits that you've received. Second, you recognize that some of the sources of this goodness are outside of yourself. Gratitude means acknowledging that other people, or even higher powers, gave us gifts, both big and small, that added goodness to your life.

We're not saying life is a bed of roses. But everyone, no matter how difficult their situation is, has received some gifts in life. And every day, no matter how difficult, has something of value. Search, and you will find.
