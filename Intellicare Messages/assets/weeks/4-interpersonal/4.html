Changing Neutral to Positive Interactions

You might find that you're life already fills packed and there's no way you can think of adding more things to do. You can still increase the number of positive interactions in your life. One way to do this is to take opportunities to turn neutral, maybe everyday interactions, into something positive. This includes chatting pleasantly with the cashier when you check out at the store, sharing a smile with a passerby on the street or a bus, or having a positive exchange with a co-worker. Changing neutral to positive interactions can sometimes help us feel a little more connected with others and increases positive emotions.
