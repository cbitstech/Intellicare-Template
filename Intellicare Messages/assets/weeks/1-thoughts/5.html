Many people find it helpful to do this in writing at first. Here's an example:

Situation

  • My boss got mad at me for not doing a job right.

Negative Automatic Thought

  • I never do anything right. I'm going to get fired.

Negative Thought Pattern

  • Discounting the positive.
  • Catastrophizing.

New, "Alternative" Thoughts

  • I've done a lot of things well.
  • Maybe there is something I can learn from this.
  • I've been at this job for a long time, and he didn't mention firing me. And even if he did, I could find another job.
