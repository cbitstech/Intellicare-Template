Rethinking Thinking (Challenging Negative Thoughts)

Just because you have a negative thought, doesn't mean you have to listen to it or agree with it. You can "rethink" those negative thoughts by coming up with new "alternative" thoughts that give you a different perspective. One way to do that is to ask yourself some questions about the negative thought:

  • Are there any other explanations?
  • Have I had any experiences that show this thought is not 100% true all the time?
  • What value is there in holding on to this rule? Does this rule help me or others?
  • Is it possible I'm blowing this out of proportion? What is a more reasonable way to look at this?
  • Have I oversimplified the issue? What is a less emotional description of the problem?
  • What is my role in this situation? Have I assigned all the blame to others?
  • If I believe this thought, what will happen? How will I feel?
  • What could be the effect of thinking about this differently?
  • If my best friend was in the situation and had this thought, what would I tell him/her?
