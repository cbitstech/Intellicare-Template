Negative Thought Pattern #5

    Magical Thinking: You make assumptions about what others are thinking (like a mind reader) or about what the future holds (like a psychic).
    • Example: "I know exactly what she's thinking."
    • Example: "If I say that, I know he'll get mad."
    • Example: "He did that on purpose."

Some of these negative thought patterns overlap. As you begin to pay attention to negative automatic thoughts, the goal is to see which patterns fit for you.
