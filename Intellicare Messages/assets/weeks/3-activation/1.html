What you do affects how you feel.

Doing more positive activities can make you feel more positive, give you a break from your problems and worries, and help you become more physically healthy.

When you are feeling down, stressed out, or anxious, you might do fewer positive activities. This is because feeling down, leads to less interest or motivation to do things, or maybe because the things you used to enjoy just don't sound as good. Also, being stressed out or anxious can make you think that you don't have time to do the things that help you to recharge mentally and physically.
