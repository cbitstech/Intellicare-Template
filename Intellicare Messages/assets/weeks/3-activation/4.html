Accomplishment Activities

Accomplishment activities are those that bring a sense of achievement or control over your life. Accomplishment is that feeling you get when you finish something, whether it's something big or something small. Accomplishment can come from something as simple as making a really great cup of coffee or something bigger, like finishing some home repairs. Crossing things off your to-do list, like finishing chores, doing the laundry, or paying bills are often great ways to get a sense of accomplishment.
