The BET strategy stands for:

Step 1: Brainstorm all possible solutions to the problem.

Hint: List every option &emdash; whether or not you think the solution is good or bad, and no matter how ridiculous or crazy the solution sounds!

Step 2: Evaluate each possible solution. Choose a solution that helps you reach your goal and has the least unwanted consequences or costs.

Hint: Some solutions could make you feel uncomfortable just because they would require you to try something new. Please don't write off solutions for that reason. Stepping out of your "comfort zone" can be a sign that you're on the right track to taking more control of your life!

Step 3: Try out the best solution! Pick a time when you will test out the solution. If the first solution does not work, repeat the BET steps and try the next best solution.

Hint: Commit to a day and time when you will try out the solution.

Throughout this week we'll be providing instructions and tips to help you use the BET steps to solve problems and improve your life. Try using the BET steps to solve a few problems this week. You may it's a useful tool that you can continue to use in your life!
