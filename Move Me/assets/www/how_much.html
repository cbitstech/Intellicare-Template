When starting a new exercise program, it is important to remember to build up over time.

If you have not been active for a while, start out slowly - you can do three 10-minute sessions in one day, instead of all 30 minutes at once, for example! MoveME can help you gradually add more time and days. Here is what we suggest:

  • Week 1: 50 minutes total
  • Week 2: 100 minutes total
  • Week 3: 150 minutes total

All adults should be getting, as recommended by the United States Department of Health and Human services, is 30-45 minutes of moderate intensity physical activity 5 days per week, or 2 hours and 30 minutes of aerobic physical activity total. Additionally, adults should engage in strengthening activities (weights, yoga, pilates, etc.) 2 times per week.

If you're already exercising but want to move more, start out by adding 5 minutes of activity to your routine per day, and MoveME will help you increase from there - and notice your overall mood increase, too!
