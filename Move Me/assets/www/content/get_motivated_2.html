Outside motivators include:

  • Choosing a start date for your new routine (think: New Year's!)
  • Taking a picture of that new swimsuit you want to fit into in June.
  • Paying yourself each time you workout - $1 per day can really add up fast!

Prompts and reminders are helpful to get you moving:

  1. Set calendar reminders.
  2. Leave your workout clothes or sneakers out as a reminder.
  3. Tell a friend about your new routine - they might want to join!
  4. Create playlists to listen to while you walk, jog, or even as you get dressed for activity.
  5. Imagine yourself at the end of your physical activity - SMILE!!!
