All physical activities can boost your mood, so the real questions are

What activities do you enjoy?

or

What activities am I willing to try?

We want you to select activities that would be both relatively fun and relatively easy to complete.

Here are some options…

  • Walking briskly
  • Biking slowly
  • Jogging
  • Swimming leisurely
  • Elliptical or other gym machines
  • Stair walking
  • Shooting baskets
  • Aerobic yoga
  • Tennis

If you're ready to increase intensity as well as your mood, you might want to try some of these activities:

  • Jogging or running
  • Sports with running (e.g., soccer)
  • Biking fast
  • Aerobic workout class
  • Hiking uphill
  • Jumping rope
  • Swimming fast
  • Rollerblading

To keep exercise interesting, you may want to try strengthening activities - remember, which ones sound interesting to you?

  • Lifting weights
  • Gym machines
  • Push-ups
  • Sit-ups
  • Working with resistance bands
  • Yoga
