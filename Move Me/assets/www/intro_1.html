When starting a new exercise program, it is important to remember to build up over time.

Duration: If you have not been active for a while, start out slowly. Break things up into 10 to 30 minute sessions. Gradually add more time and days.

We suggest:

  • Week 1: 50 minutes total.
  • Week 2: 100 minutes total.
  • Week 3: 200 minutes total.

Target: 200 minutes per week.
