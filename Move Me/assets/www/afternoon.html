Here are some common motivation-sapping thoughts for afternoon exercise. Have you had any of these thoughts before?

  • I am working well, I shouldn't stop
  • I am too busy now
  • I have to finish this now
  • I will get just one more thing done
  • I better not take a break till later
  • I need to digest lunch first
  • I don't have time right now

Remember that afternoon exercise may enhance your problem-solving levels while reducing your stress.

Here is some more positive self-talk instead:

  • I am taking a break for some activity to have a fresh mind for my afternoon job demands.
  • I know that I feel different after exercise; let me see what work feels like under those conditions.
  • Activity helps me manage stress and boost my mood - and is one way I am helping myself be more productive.
