Here are some common motivation-sapping thoughts for evening exercise. Have you had any of these thoughts today?

  • I am too tired
  • It is about to get dark
  • It will be too cold
  • I just ate dinner and need to digest
  • I need to get to sleep early
  • I can just put it off until morning

Here are some tips and tricks to help you manage these thoughts:

  • Consider going straight to the gym from work/school.
  • If you go home, avoid your low motivation zones (i.e., couch, bed).
  • Put your workout clothes on as soon as you get home. Don’t sit - just get out the door!

Remember that evening exercise can be a terrific way to close out the day and prepare for a relaxed and enjoyable evening.
