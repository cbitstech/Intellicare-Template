Here are some common motivation-sapping thoughts for morning exercise. Have you had any of these thoughts before?

  • I'll skip my workout just this one time
  • It is too cold to get out of bed
  • Sleep will be better for my mood
  • Missing my workout once won't matter
  • I'll just turn over in bed once more
  • 10 more minutes in bed won't matter
  • I am too tired to exercise well
  • I can always exercise this evening

Remind yourself of these advantages of early morning exercise:

  • Exercise is an excellent way to start the day
  • Early morning time is excellent "me" time
  • All day you get to enjoy the sense of accomplishment of having already exercised

Remember that you made your decision to exercise when you had an AWAKE MIND the previous evening!
