Welcome to the Movement Motion Enhancer (MoveME).

People often engage in physical activity for health and/or fitness benefits. However, there are mental health benefits of physical activity.

Exercise improves our mood, memory, concentration, and sleep.

A crucial component, however, is motivation. It is difficult to get in the habit of exercising.

MoveME helps suggest how much exercise is appropriate to boost your mood, provides tips for motivation, and tracks your progress towards your goals.
