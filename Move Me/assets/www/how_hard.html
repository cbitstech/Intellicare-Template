GREAT news: You can boost your mood and your health through regular moderate physical activity! For most people a brisk walk is enough to reach moderate intensity.

How much you enjoy exercise can change with how intensely you exercise. A few things to keep in mind:

  • Moderate exercise for you is the level where you can have a conversation while you are active.
  • Comfort with an exercise routine increases the chances of sticking to that routine, especially when its a new routine.
  • Increasing the intensity of your activity for short periods can help you build endurance in a comfortable way.
