As compared to searching inside, we are asking you to look outward for influences on your motivation.

External Influence:

  • Motivations are influenced by a wide range of external events.
  • For example, if a friend states a motivation (I really want to get in better shape) you may find similar motivations moving to the top of your list.
  • Motivations are also affected by mood. If you become frustrated, motivation for whatever goal you are pursuing decreases, and other motivations compete for your attention.

Part of success in staying motivated to exercise has to do with modifying your environment to change your motivation.
