We rely on different people for different things. Research shows that people who support us in positive ways tend to fall in 4 different categories:

Practical Support [P] - Who will drive you to the hospital? Lend you money?

Advice / Information [A] - Who do you go to for help when you don't know how to do something? When you don't feel well? When you need help navigating a tricky situation?

Companionship [C] - Who do you walk around a park with? Who would spend a quiet afternoon with you? Who shares your tears and your joys?

Emotional Support [E] - Who do you go to for encouragement? understanding? to share your feelings? to help lift your mood?

Some people are great at supporting us in more ways than one - they can fall into multiple categories and are probably very good friends.
