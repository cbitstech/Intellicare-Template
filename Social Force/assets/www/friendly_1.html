Active listening is a process of focusing on understanding what the other person is saying and communicating your understanding with both verbal and nonverbal responses.

Here are some helpful tips to becoming an active listener:

  • Make eye contact with the person you are speaking with - this will help you pay attention to the person
  • Try to focus on what the other person is saying… not how you will respond
  • Nod occasionally
  • Smile! Feel free to show facial expressions
  • Reflect on what you heard by paraphrasing, starting with a phrase like "What I'm hearing is…" or "It sounds like…"
  • Ask questions about things that are unclear
  • Use nonjudgmental words or phrases to reflect back
