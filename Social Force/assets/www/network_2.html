The easiest way to meet people is to do something you like doing in the company of others.

Since the main focus is the activity you are doing, not just meeting others, there will be less pressure on you than in a setting where the whole purpose is to meet people. That sounds just plain stressful!

Even if you don't find anyone in particular that you enjoy, you will still have been doing something pleasant, something you like, and you won't feel like you wasted your time.
