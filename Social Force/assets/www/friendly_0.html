Here are some tips and tricks to starting a conversation with someone - anyone you know or don't know!

  • Introduce yourself, say hello!
  • Remark on the location or the occasion, whatever you're doing
  • Ask open-ended questions - "what do you think about [subject]?" or "how was your day?"
  • Keep the conversation going with small talk
  • Say the other person's name now and then
  • Use the words the other person says when making comments - if they call a marker a crayon, call it a crayon too!
  • Use "sensing" words - "I feel you" or "I can tell" help to convey that you're engaged
  • Practice, practice, practice!
