• What if I cannot remember how an exercise works?

    Click the info button on the top right of the screen. You will be able to review the instructions.

  • I forgot why a genre of exercises (i.e. deep breathing) is helpful. How can I revisit the information shown to me the first time I loaded those exercises?

    The info button at the top of the exercise list will take you to the genre information page.

  • Can I leave the app while the audio file is playing?

    Yes. You will be able to control the audio from the system tray. You can access the tray by pulling down from the top of the screen.

  • I have left the app while the audio is playing, how do I return to the app?

    Open the system tray by pulling down from the top of your phone screen, and press on the title of the track that appears.

  • I have left the app while the audio is playing, how do I stop and start the audio?

    Open the system tray by pulling down the top of your phone screen, and press the "play" or "pause" icon that appears next to the track title.

  • Will I be prompted to use Purple Chill?

    Yes, if you set a reminder. You can set a reminder by accessing "settings" in the menu at the top right of the main screen.

  • How do I change the timing of the reminder?

    From the top right menu in the main screen, select "settings." Then select "schedule reminder." A popup will appear that allows you to set the time and days of the week to be reminded to use the app. Tap "schedule" to complete the setting.

  • I have used all the different exercises, will there be more available? If so, how will I know more are available?

    New exercises may be available in future updates. The Intellicare Conductor will alert you when updates to Purple Chill are available.

  • How do I send suggestions or questions to the developers?

    This can be done right from the app. Just click the menu button in the top right corner of the screen and choose "Feedback". Now just choose how you would like to send the information, type away, and send it off.
