Purple Chill

"Purple Chill" is an app designed to teach you skills to help you relax during stressful situations. You can use this app when you are stressed to help you calm down or even when you're not stressed to practice skills and get better at using them. "Purple Chill" contains several audio files to guide you through relaxation skills. Because of this, we suggest finding a comfortable place to listen to these files.

"Purple Chill" is a growing app! We'll also ask you some brief questions to help us learn more over time about what's helpful for you.
