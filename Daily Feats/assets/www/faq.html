1. Do I just check the "Feats" that I accomplished?

    Yes, only check the "Feats" you have accomplished that day.

  2. I have finished checking off the "Feats" I accomplished today. What do I do now?

    Great! That's it. You're done. You can hit the home button to go back to your phone's home page or hit the back button to go back to Intellicare.

  3. How do I know Daily Feats saved my answers?

    Daily Feats saves your responses automatically. You can click in and out of the application at any time and it will save your progress.

  4. What if I forget what I am supposed to do?

    No problem at all. Just click on the menu button in the top right corner of the screen and choose "Help / Rules".

  5. I want to look at a past month to check the "Feats" from a specific day. How do I do that?

    You can definitely check past months "Feats". Just drag your finger across the screen from left to right on the calendar and the calendar will go back one month. Swipe again to keep going. Just choose the date you want to check and it will show you what you accomplished.

  6. I want to view all possible "Feats" when I choose what I have accomplished today. How do I do that?

    In the top right corner of the screen, click the menu button. Click the first option listed, "Edit Feats List". On this screen you can check any of the "Feats" you would like to appear on the calendar screen, or uncheck those you don't want to appear. Only checked "Feats" will appear.

  7. I want to add a "Feat" to the options of what I have accomplished today. How do I do that?

    Perfect! Go to "Edit Feats List" (see #6). In the top right corner click "Add Feat". Here you can type in any "Feat" you would like. Make sure you check the "Feats" you want to appear on the calendar screen. Only checked "Feats" will appear.

  8. How do I change the time Daily Feats reminds me to check in?

    Click on the menu button, in the top right corner of the screen. Choose the third option "Settings". You should see, at the top of the list, "Daily Reminder Time". Click on this, change the time of the reminder and click "Done". All set!

  9. How do I send suggestions or questions to the developers?

    This can be done right from the app. Just click the menu button in the top right corner of the screen and choose "Feedback". Now just choose how you would like to send the information, type away, and send it off.
