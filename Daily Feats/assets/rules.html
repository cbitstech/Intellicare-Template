When you started using Daily Feats, you answered a questionnaire that assessed your depression level on a scale from 1 to 4 (1 = most depressed, 4 = least depressed).

To advance in Daily Feats, the goal is to raise your level by completing daily feats in a consistent manner to establish a "streak".

For a given level, there are a certain number of associated feats. If you complete more than half the feats for a given level for five (5) consecutive days, you will "level-up" and be able to work on new feats.

However, if you don't complete more than half the feats for your level for five (5) consecutive days, your level will drop and you will need to work on the lower-level feats to resume progressing.

Good luck working towards reaching Level 4 (and staying there)!
