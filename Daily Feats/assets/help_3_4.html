Based on your answer to how you are feeling, Daily Feats will start you with the following list of goals:

  • I accomplished something today.
  • I enjoyed an activity today.
  • I took care of myself.
  • I was grateful for something.
  • I overcame a bad habit.
  • I strengthened a good habit.
  • I faced something I wanted to avoid.
  • I avoided something I wish I had faced.

Once a day, ideally at the end of the day, you will be asked to evaluate how many of these goals you completed that day.

The purpose of completing this checklist is to give you an achievable set of goals to work towards, and to help you keep them in mind on a daily basis.
